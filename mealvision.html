cloud based 3D precision food intake monitoring

Mealvision™

Mealvision™ is used for monitoring food intake by capturing a precision 3D image of a user's plate before and after they have had their meal.

The user's plate also has a chip that the Mealvision™ detects and connects the captured images of the plate to said user and stores this information on the cloud. This device can be used in aged care facilities, schools, gyms etc.
